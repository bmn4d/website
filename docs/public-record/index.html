Public Record

Embracing a decentralized web naturally highlights the need for a Public Record – a shared record of the information that has been published by the plethora of entities on the web. A key aspect of the public record is to provide a basis for knowing who produced certain information and when they produced it. Ideally this aggregate dataset, the public record, should be stored and updated in a decentralized fashion.
